## What Is Conversational AI? `[LT: what is conversational ai]`

Conversational AI `[PK]` is the technology that enables machines to understand, process, and respond to human language in natural, contextual dialogue — across text and voice channels. It combines natural language processing (NLP), large language models (LLMs), retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and dialogue management to create AI systems that can hold meaningful, multi-turn conversations with users.

The critical distinction is between **rule-based chatbots** and **conversational AI**. Rule-based chatbots follow pre-programmed decision trees — if a user says X, respond with Y. They break when inputs vary, cannot handle ambiguity, and feel robotic. Conversational AI `[PK]` interprets intent from freeform language, maintains context across a conversation, retrieves real-time information from business systems, and generates dynamic responses that feel natural and helpful.

| **Maintenance** | Constant manual rule updates | Model tuning + knowledge base refresh |
