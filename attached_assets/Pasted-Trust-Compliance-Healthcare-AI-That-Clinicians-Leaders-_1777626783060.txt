Trust & Compliance
Healthcare AI That Clinicians, Leaders, and Regulators Can Trust
Every AGIX healthcare AI system is governed by six non-negotiable principles — built in from day one, not added as an afterthought.

Our 6 Non-Negotiable Principles
1
Human-in-the-Loop by Design

AI assists decisions — it never replaces clinical judgment. Every output is a recommendation, not an order.

2
Explainability at Every Step

Every recommendation includes source citations and confidence scores. No black-box outputs.

3
No Autonomous Clinical Actions

No diagnosis, treatment, prescription, or escalation happens without human review and approval.

4
Audit-Ready Architecture

Every AI action is logged, timestamped, traceable, and reviewable. Full accountability chain.

5
Privacy & Consent First

Patient data is protected, access-controlled, and purpose-limited. Never used for external model training.

6
Fail-safe Design

When AI is uncertain, it says so — and escalates to a human. AI fails safely, never silently.
